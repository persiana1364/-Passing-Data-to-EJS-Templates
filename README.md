## How it Works

1. **Enter Your Name**: Type in your first and last name into the input fields and click "OK".
2. **The App Responds**: The app processes your name and calculates the number of characters in your full name.
3. **Results**: The app displays the total number of characters in your full name. If either name field is left empty, you'll be prompted to fill them in.
